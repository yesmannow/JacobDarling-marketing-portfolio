## ðŸ’¡ Key Takeaways

### What Makes a Portfolio Site Work for Job Search:

1. **Lead with outcomes, not process**
   - Hiring managers care about results first
   - Show metrics prominently
   - Prove you can deliver value

2. **Make scanning easy**
   - Use visual hierarchy effectively
   - Break up text with bullets and subheads
   - Make important info impossible to miss

3. **Build trust quickly**
   - Specific metrics are more believable than vague claims
   - Testimonials from real people with titles
   - Show your process and thinking

4. **Match their mental model**
   - Use language from job descriptions
   - Address their specific pain points
   - Show you understand their business challenges

5. **Remove friction**
   - Multiple clear CTAs
   - Easy ways to contact you
   - Set clear expectations

### Remember:

**You have 30-90 seconds** to convince a hiring manager you're worth interviewing.

**Every word should answer:** "Why should I hire this person?"

**Every number should prove:** "This person delivers results."

**Every section should lead to:** "I should reach out to learn more."
